r/Nostalgia ||Vikingthemad

    Probably sounds weird but I really miss the old internet. Like pre 2010 internet, maybe its jut because I grew up with it but I remember it having so much more charm and interest to it, even though things were lower quality and lies were common. Just to give some examples: Old youtube videos about cats, Daft Hands, Mean Kitty song, even old video game walkthrough. Yeah walkthroughs, not lets plays. Older memes like rage comics, anything with a splash of MS paint, the many -cats (Long cat, monorail cat, hover cat, basement cat, ceiling cat, etc). Stupid yeah but they never felt forced. Even advice animals that didn't have like three lines of scrunched up text. Nothing was quite clean and it all had a bit of grime to it, picture hosting was a little fiddly but was really cool to see on forums, text art, low quality assets instead of being too clean, not the best layouts until you learn them, etc. Hell even myspace I got a little nostalgia for since it was probably the most customizable social media to ever exist. Never used mine much though. Oh and google not owning everything and spying on you. I can't be the only like this can I? It really sucks since theres no real way to go all the way back like you can with physical things. You can't dig up an old version of a forum that isn't flooded with people brought in by the era of smartphones and tablets.
